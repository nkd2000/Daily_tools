Overview

This privacy policy explains the types of information this site may collect and how it is used. We respect your privacy and strive to keep the experience safe and useful. Our tools are designed to process data locally in your browser whenever possible.

Data and Tracking

We do not require accounts to use the tools. However, some pages use third-party services such as Google AdSense.

  • Cookies: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Advertising: Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

ads.txt Authorization

We provide an ads.txt file to declare authorized sellers for advertising inventory. This helps prevent unauthorized selling of inventory and ensures transparency. You can view the file at /ads.txt.
